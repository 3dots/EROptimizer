            Equipment & Magic / Armor
              +

            Gauntlets in Elden Ring is an armor piece that is worn to protect the player's hands from damage. It is speculated that each piece of armor has different defense and resistance values while equipping a piece along with its proper set provides the player with certain bonuses that can aid in different combat situations.
