            Armor Set 41.6 Weight

            Banished Knight Set is an Armor Set in Elden Ring. Banished Knight Set is a Heavyweight armor. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.

             

            Where to find Banished Knight Set in Elden Ring

            Chance of any of its pieces to drop by Banished Knight enemies. These can be found at:

            • Stormveil Castle. Elden Ring Map Link.
            • Cathedral of Dragon Communion.

             

            Banished Knight Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring

            • Banished Knight Helm.
            • Banished Knight Armor.
            • Banished Knight Gauntlets
            • Banished Knight Greaves.

             

            Banished Knight Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips

            • If you want to farm this armor Set. You can port to "Rampart Tower"-Grace in Stormveil Castle. Go back to the stairs and go down, there is Banished Knight with a Halberd. He respawns.
            • There are confirmed varients of Helm and Chest part.

                                                  07 Mar 2022 00:20  

                                                  After some extensive testing it appears to be quite a rare drop for the un-altered chest piece with cape and tabard. There are two places you can get it, both of them are in Castle Sol in the Mountain Top of the Giants Area. One spot is by the Commander O'Niel bossfight, a halberd wielding knight carries it just below the boss area accessable after leaping from the nearby elevator onto the nearby rampart below. The second place (and quite a bit more of a hassle) is the dual wielding red-eyed knight found by progressing through Church of the Eclipse Grace to the nearby ladder to the South that leads you up the castle's walls. The second fight is much harder to repeat due to the moveset of this knight. Both knights have a drop-rate for the chest piece equal to roughly 2-3% after killing both knights each 100 times and having it drop three times and two times respectively. Hope this info helps!
