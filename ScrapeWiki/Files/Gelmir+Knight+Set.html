            Armor Set 28.1Weight

            Gelmir Knight Set is an Armor Set in Elden Ring. Gelmir Knight Set an Armor piece set that still has not lost its luster. It bears an emblem that none wear any longer, standing as it does for a lord that fell from loft ambition into gluttonous depravity. As the lord lost his dignity, so too did these knights lose their master. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.

             

            Where to find Gelmir Knight Set in Elden Ring

            Gelmir Hero's Grave dungeon

             

            Gelmir Knight Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring

            • Gelmir Knight Helm
            • Gelmir Knight Armor
            • Gelmir Knight Gauntlets
            • Gelmir Knight Greaves

             

            Gelmir Knight Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips

            • There is only one Gelmir Knight, and he is a slain NPC located in the Gelmir Hero's Grave dungeon
            • The Gelmir Knight's corpse (with full armor to loot) is guarded by a single Bloodhound Knight
            • There are two massive meat grinder/ wheel trap vehicle areas; the Bloodhound Knight guard and slain Gelmir Knight hero can be accessed on the second wheel trap area by falling off the edge of the bottom third of the ramp (but still hugging the edge you fell off)

                                So i think i figured this armour set out:
                                There are actually 4 different variants: 3 associated with 3 different groups and the 4th belonging to a deceased group

                                First one is the Godrick Knight armor
                                The metal plating is bronze while the tabard orange and green and shows a heraldic Tree and i think a griffin
                                Set pieces drop very rarely from the equally rare Godrick Knights which are located outside of Stormveil Castle

                                Second one is the Cuckoo Knight armor
                                Silver metal with a red and blue tabard, showing a random bird wing and i think a raven too
                                The helmet on top of having a plume, also has a massive feather on the forehead too
                                These drop from the Raya Lucaria Knights which are pretty common near the Academy itself
                                Best farming spot is the East Raya Lucaria Gate grace which is northeast from the academy because there are 4 isolated mounted knight patrolling different sections of the forest and one dismounted knight who is accompanied by resting soldiers

                                Third one is the Leyndell Knight armor
                                Golden metal and the tabard is a pure bright yellow and only shows the eldtree instead of two things at once
                                The head piece looks the most different from the other variants: Instead of a plume, the helmet got a massive metal head piece which i think shows the eldtree too
                                Pieces from the Leyndell Knights which are everywhere in the Capital

                                Fourth set and the one which is the wiki page about is the Gelmir Knight Set
                                Associated with the Praetor Rykard who resides on Mount Gelmir, everything went to **** at that place and except a few foot soldiers, his whole army is gone, included all knights
                                The metal is a classic metallic grey and the tabard is a very dark grey, possible even black while it shows some kind of lines instead of an animal or the eldtree
                                Since all the Knights are gone, you can only find the set inside The Gelmir Hero's Grave catacomb (Which is a brutal place by the way)
                                The whole set will be behind a single Bloodhound Knight who drops his own armour too by the way
