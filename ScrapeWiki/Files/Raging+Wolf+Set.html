            Armor Set 24.1 Total Weight

            Raging Wolf Set is an Armor Set in Elden Ring. The Raging Wolf Set is a Medium Weight armor tailored to Dexterity characters. Armor provides our character with protection and different resistance against all types of damage and negative status effects. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.

             

            Armor worn by Vargram the Bloody Wolf, one of the first Tarnished to visit the Roundtable Hold.

            Where to find Raging Wolf Set in Elden Ring

            You can find the Raging Wolf Set in:

            • Leyndell, Royal Capital. After obtaining the second to last letter from Volcano Manor, you will be sent to assist Knight Bernahl with assassinating Vargram the Raging Wolf and Errant Sorcerer Wilhelm. On successful invasion, you will receive the full Raging Wolf Set. See it on the Elden Ring Map here.

             

            Raging Wolf Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring

            In Elden Ring, the Bloody Wolf Set is composed of 4 different armor pieces. These are the following: 

            • Raging Wolf Helm
            • Raging Wolf Armor
            • Raging Wolf Gauntlets
            • Raging Wolf Greaves

             

            Elden Ring Raging Wolf Set Notes and Tips

            • This armor set was worn by the Bloody Wolf class in the Elden Ring Closed Network Test.
            • Can no longer be acquired after defeating Maliketh, the boss of Crumbling Farum Azula or after defeating Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy.
