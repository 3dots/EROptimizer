            Royal Knight Helm is a Helm in Elden Ring. Royal Knight Helm is part of the Royal Knight Set Armor Set, and DESCRIBE ITEM HERE. Royal Knight Helm protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.

             

            Silver helm of Loretta, a Knight who served Miquella's Haligtree.

            Loretta, once a royal Carian Knight, went on a journey in search of a haven for Albinaurics, and determined that the Haligtree was their best chance for eventual salvation. 

             

            Where to find Royal Knight Helm in Elden Ring

            The Royal Knight Helm Helm can be found at:

            • Can be brought from Finger Reader Enia, at Roundtable Hold after defeating Royal Knight Loretta for  4000.

             

            Royal Knight Helm Set in Elden Ring

            • Royal Knight Helm is a piece of the Royal Knight Set
            • The rest of the set is composed by the Royal Knight Armor, Royal Knight Gauntlets and Royal Knight Greaves.
