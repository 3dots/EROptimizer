            Equipment & Magic / Armor
              +

            Chest Armor in Elden Ring is an armor piece that is worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It is speculated that each piece of armor has different defense and resistance values while equipping a piece along with its proper set provides the player with certain bonuses that can aid in different combat situations.
