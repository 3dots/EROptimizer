            Armor Set 63 Weight

            Bull-Goat Set is an Armor Set in Elden Ring. Bull-Goat Set Great Horned Tragoth's Armor Set. Covers its wearer with a pair of giant horns, providing staunch poise. Tragoth is a famed knight of assistance. Countless Tarnished, facing adversity in the Lands Between, have survived thanks only to the Great Horned One's aid.  Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.

             

            Where to find Bull-Goat Set in Elden Ring

            Once Patches has moved to Volcano Manor during his questline, he will give you a letter to kill Great Horned Tragoth. Note that you need to join the Volcano Manor covenant and do a kill mission first for Patches to show up and give you this kill mission. You get the armor set immediately after killing Tragoth. Map Link

            Bull-Goat Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips

            • Set has the highest poise and physical defense in the game, but also the heaviest.

                                      PSA: Do you care about poise? Then you don't need to worry about this set.

                                      Despite having the highest poise in the game, it's also the heaviest set in the game (100 poise for 63 weight - 1.59 poise/weight ratio). You'll need very high END if you want to wear this with a heavy weapon, and you'll probably have to take points from other stats that could be more beneficial to you.

                                      You only need 103 poise as it's the upper softcap for permanent poise that comes from equipment (at that level, you can tank 2 light attacks or a medium attack, and in PvP you'll be able to tank a 2-handed halberd R1). To get this much poise, simply equip the Veteran's set and the Bull-Goat talisman (79 poise for 45 weight - 1.75 poise/weight ratio / Do note that to get above 100 poise you will need to use the talisman anyway). You'll have 105 poise which is only 2 points higher than what you need in terms of poise. You'll have much more equip load to play around with, and you'll be able to put points away from END into other stats that matter.
