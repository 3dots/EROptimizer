            Deathbed Smalls is a Leg Armor in Elden Ring. Deathbed Smalls is part of the Deathbed Set Armor Set, and seem to be cut from the final release of the game. Deathbed Smalls protect the player's legs by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.
