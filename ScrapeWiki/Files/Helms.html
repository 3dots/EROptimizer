            Equipment & Magic / Armor
              +

            Helms in Elden Ring is an armor piece that is worn on the head for protection. It is speculated that each piece of armor has different defense and resistance values while equipping a piece along with its proper set provides the player with certain bonuses that can aid in different combat situations.

            Many Helms have Special Effects. See Equipment with Special Effects for a list of items with unique properties that affect your stats or performance.
