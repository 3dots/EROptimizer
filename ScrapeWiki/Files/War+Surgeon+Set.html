            The War Surgeon Set is an Armor Set in Elden Ring. It is the cloth set worn by Vare. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.

             

            Where to find War Surgeon Set in Elden Ring

            • Dropped by killing one of three Nameless White Mask NPC invaders in the Mohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum area of Siofra River, northwest of the Palace Approach Ledge-Road site of grace, in the blood filled lake. This one can be found adjacent to the Giant Crow enemy near the blood filled lake drop off.

             

            War Surgeon Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring

            • White Mask
            • War Surgeon Gown
            • War Surgeon Gloves
            • War Surgeon Trousers

             

            War Surgeon Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips

            • The White Mask piece increases the players attack power power by 10% for 20 seconds once blood loss has been inflicted on an enemy.
            • This chest piece is initially dropped in its (altered) form, removing the neck drape and lowering its defensive properties. This can be undone at any site of grace so long as you have Tailoring Tools, or for free at Boc The Seamster.
            • Killing Mohg, Lord of Blood will stop the Nameless White Mask invaders from spawning, locking the player out of obtaining this set.
