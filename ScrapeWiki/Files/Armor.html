            Equipment & Magic
              +

            Armor in Elden Ring is a protective gear that is worn by the player's character which grants various bonus effects and protection and resistances from damages and status effects. In Elden Ring, armor is divided into pieces such as Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor. Equipping a set of armor grants a player buffs, but players can also mix and match armor pieces to obtain a certain percentage of defensive properties.

            Many Armor pieces have Special Effects. See Equipment with Special Effects for a list of items with unique properties that affect your stats or performance.

             

            All Armor Sets in Elden Ring

            Armor Sets are complete combinations of individual Armor in Elden Ring. Armor Sets provides players with protection and resistances against all types of damage such as Magic, Fire, Light or Holy. Wearing Armor also changes the character's appearance. Wearing all Set pieces won't grant any additional effects.

                          I've found that most if not all humanoid enemies, aside from Invaders, have a chance to drop pieces to the armor set they're wearing. For example, the guardians of Minor Erdtrees drop pieces to the Guardian set (Guardian Mask, Bracers, etc) and Gold Dragon Cult Knights drop pieces to the Leyndell Knight Set, all of this as well as their weapons. A good rule of thumb; If you see some nice armor, try spending some time farming that mob. Chances are, if they're humanoid, you'll get it.
