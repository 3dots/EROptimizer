            Armor Set 35.7 Weight

            Cleanrot Set is an Armor Set in Elden Ring. The Cleanrot Set has high immunity and robustness resistance, as well as good physical and elemental damage absorption. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.

             

            Where to find Cleanrot Set in Elden Ring

            • Pieces drop individually from Cleanrot Knights in Caelid, specifically in Aeonia Swamp, War-Dead Catacombs.
            • Cleanrot Knight farming locations around Inner Aeonia: (most knights take a moment to spawn, rising up from the swamp)
            • Pieces also drop individually from Cleanrot Knights in Miquella's Haligtree and The Shaded Castle

            Cleanrot Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring

            • Cleanrot Helm
            • Cleanrot Armor
            • Cleanrot Gauntlets
            • Cleanrot Greaves

             

            Cleanrot Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips

            • A good place to grind the set is the prayer room site of grace in Miquella's Haligtree.
